<h2>Running Panopset Desktop Applications</h2>

<h3>Using a platform specific installer.</h3>

<p>
Substitute flywheel, with the <a href="https://panopset.com">Panopset</a> application name of your choice.
Here are the
default paths to the Panopset Flywheel application, for these PC platforms:
</p>

<h2>Linux</h2>
<pre>
/opt/panopset/bin/flywheel
</pre>
<h2>Macintosh</h2>
<pre>
/Applications/panopset.app/Contents/MacOS/flywheel
</pre>
<h2>Windows</h2>
<pre>
&quot;c:\Program Files\panopset\flywheel.exe&quot;
</pre>
&nbsp;


<h3>Using your own JRE.</h3>
<p>If you download the panopset.jar file for your platform, or build it, you would need your own JRE.
See the <a href="build.html">build</a> page, for details.</p>
