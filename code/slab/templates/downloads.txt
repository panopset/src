<h2>Running...</h2>

<h3>Using a platform specific installer.</h3>

<p>
Substitute flywheel, in the <b>Launch path</b> for your platform listed above,
with the Panopset <a href="./product.html">application</a> name of your choice.
</p>

<h3>Using your own JRE.</h3>
<p>
If you download the panopset.jar file for your platform, or <a href="build.html">build</a> it,
place panopset.jar in your home directory, and do the same except for the <b>Java command</b> for your platform,
as listed above.
</p>


<h2>versions</h2>

<p>
help-&gt;about, from any Panopset desktop application, will give you the <a href="https://github.com/panopset/${GN}/blob/main/docs/vh.md">version</a>.
